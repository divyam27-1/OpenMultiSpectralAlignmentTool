For stateless worker
PYTHONPATH  worker.py   workflow    plan_path   chunk_idx   [rest of args are positionless]

For persistent worker
PYTHONPATH  persistent_worker.py    plan_path   [rest of args are passed by ZMQ]
- Payloads:
    1) Heartbeat: No Payload
    2) TaskStart: [workflow, chunk_idx, image_idx]
    3) TaskFinish: [TaskFinishCodes], Upstream-only message
    4) WorkerFlush: No Payload
    5) WorkerStop: No Payload
    6) WorkerError: [WorkerErrorCodes]
